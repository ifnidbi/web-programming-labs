    About

    Dexter Morgan is fictional character from the book and TV series "Dexter". He is a Blood Spatter analyst that works for Miami PD Homicide. Secretly, he's a serial killer that murders and disappears other murderers. When he was just three years old he had to watch his mother be chopped up with a chainsaw. The responding officer Harry Morgan adopted him. As Dexter grew up, his father saw that he was not normal. Harry taught him a code to live by in order to fit in satisfy his urge to kill. Dexter grew up and joined Miami PD in order to have inside information on the murderers that he plans to kill.
